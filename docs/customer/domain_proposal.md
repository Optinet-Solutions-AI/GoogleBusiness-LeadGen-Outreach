## Why a domain matters

Your website lives on a domain — the address customers type to find you. Right now your demo is at a temporary preview URL like `joes-plumbing.pages.dev`. That's fine for showing you the site, but for the live, professional version you want **your own name on it**, like:

- `joesplumbingatx.com`
- `joesplumbing.co`
- `joes-plumbing-austin.com`

Three reasons it matters:

1. **Trust.** A real domain looks legitimate. A `pages.dev` URL doesn't.
2. **SEO.** Google ranks branded domains higher than generic subdomains for local searches.
3. **Email.** A custom domain unlocks `you@joesplumbing.com` instead of `joesplumbing@gmail.com`.

## Three ways we can do this

Pick whichever fits your situation. We handle the technical setup either way.

### Option 1 — You already own a domain
Great. Tell us the domain name; we attach it to your website in about 5 minutes. **You do nothing technical.** Your DNS provider may need one record changed — we send you the exact value to paste, with a screenshot of where it goes.

### Option 2 — You want one but don't have one
We register it for you at cost. Most `.com` domains are about **$11/year**. We'll suggest 3–5 options based on your business name and reserve whichever you pick. After purchase the domain is **yours** — registered under your name, with you as the legal owner. We just keep the technical wiring.

### Option 3 — You want to use an existing domain you registered yourself
Same as Option 1. Send us the login (read-only) for your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.) so we can update the DNS, or we'll send you the records to paste.

## Common questions

**Q: Do I "own" the website?**
You own the **content** (your business info, photos, branding) and the **domain**. The hosting and the technical site live with us — same way you don't own your phone tower, you own your phone number. If you ever leave us, we'll hand you everything (HTML, images, text) and help you point your domain elsewhere.

**Q: What if I already have a website (e.g. WordPress, Wix, Squarespace)?**
We'll ask for read-only access while we build, but you keep your existing site live until launch day. On launch day we point your domain at the new site. Old site can stay parked at the old URL or be retired — your call.

**Q: What if I want to take it over later?**
Fine. We'll export the entire site as HTML/CSS files and send them to you, plus your domain login. You can host it yourself anywhere (Cloudflare Pages, GitHub Pages, Netlify — all free) or with another agency.

**Q: Can my customers email me at the domain?**
Yes. Setting up `you@yourdomain.com` requires a separate email service (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, or similar — usually $6–10/user/month). We'll wire the DNS records but the email itself is on a separate plan from us.

**Q: Will my old website's Google ranking transfer?**
Yes if we 301-redirect the old URLs to the matching new ones — we do this as part of launch. Most customers see equal-or-better rankings within 30–60 days because the new site loads dramatically faster.
